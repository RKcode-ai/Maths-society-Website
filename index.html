Information & Rules

Open to all levels — whether you’re just discovering proofs or already playing with Olympiad-style problems.

Core competition rules (11:00–16:00)

  • Closed-book: no calculators, phones, tablets, notes, or digital references.
  • Work individually — no discussion or collaboration.
  • Show all reasoning: partial credit depends on clear working.
  • Submit your answers before 16:00; late submissions cannot be accepted.
